ABOUT

Shalom & Welcome!...
At Kakamega Yearly Meeting of Friends Church (Quakers), we warmly invite you to journey with us in faith, rooted in the timeless Quaker values of Simplicity, Peace, Integrity, Community, Equality, and Stewardship (SPICES). Together, we grow in love, serve with purpose, and live out the light of Christ in every action.
